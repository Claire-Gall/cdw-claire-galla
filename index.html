D3 Structure Investigation

Integrating temporal and relational structures into my workflow, with specific attention to my precedent study on relational touchpoints within architectural space as directed by a spatial artist.

Nodes of Participation: Rafael Lozano-Hemmer 'Under Scan'

This temporal structure contextualizes Rafael's installation work with the works that preceded his practice. His work is situated within a context of fleeting architectural interventions.

Temporal Structure 1

In these two visualizations, I am generating a relational structure that speculates on how Rafael Lozano-Hemmer could have foresaw his works participants interacting with the piece relative to how it could have actually played out based on a series of factors. Those being the level of stimulation the piece caused in inspiring play amongst participants of Under Scan or in the case of Voz Alta, a sense of remembrance and inclination to speak.
