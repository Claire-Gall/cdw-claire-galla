Under Scan: Predicted Relational Structure

In these two relational structures, I am generating a relational structure that corresponds to Rafael Lozano-Hemmer's practice in the piece Voz Alta and Under Scan, each in their own right. Under Scan creates a tri-directional relationship between partipant shadows and physical touchpoints within the 1,000-2,000 sq. meter space. The visual language I use is meant to create a spatial narrative of how the physical space was activated through the objects of Voz Alta or Under Scan during a finite period of time.
