Visualizing the temporal movement of installation participants of piece 'Under Scan'

Cadence of Participant Movement

In these two visualizations, I am generating a relational structure that speculates on how Rafael Lozano-Hemmer could have foresaw his works participants interacting with the piece relative to how it could have actually played out based on a series of factors. Those being the level of stimulation the piece caused in inspiring play amongst participants of Under Scan or in the case of Voz Alta, a sense of remembrance and inclination to speak. The first relational structure looks at the moments participants triggered a shadow and video response from the surrounding motion sensors.
